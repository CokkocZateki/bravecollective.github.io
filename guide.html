Don't worry about hauling your stuff
Frankly, your stuff weighs more than it's worth paying someone to carry it. Leave it all behind besides the ship you're flying in and some ammo. If you find you really need it later, you can always go back for it.
Do not, I repeat, do not use corp mail.
We have several thousand people in the corp, if everyone spammed corp mail for every little thing it would turn into a mess very quickly.
