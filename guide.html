Unfuck your overview!

Download Sarah's Overview Pack and follow the instructions found on the page. It is important that you not only install the pack but also follow the instructions to make sure all people have the correct color in your overview
This ensures that you don't shoot friendlies, and you can reasonably tell when it's safe to shoot enemies. It's important.
Note that on the main PvP tab, you won't be able to see friendlies. In your frigate roams with 20 of your best friends, you don't really want them hiding important information. Besides, they're still on tab 4 if you want to see that they're near.
There's a few caveats such as learning how to see drones, loot cans, and planets. You can ask about that in the dojo.

Come out to Sendaya!

You could die on the way. If you're in a frigate, shuttle, or pod, you'll probably be fine. The best way to experience eve is to dive in head first. You don't have that much to lose at this point anyway. Just make sure you have a clone (and if you did the tutorial missions, it would have walked you through this).

Don't worry about hauling your stuff

Frankly, your stuff weighs more than it's worth paying someone to carry it. Leave it all behind besides the ship you're flying in and some ammo. If you find you really need it later, you can always go back for it.
If you really know what you're doing, and you're certain you want stuff hauled: More Info

Do not, I repeat, do not use corp mail.

We have several thousand people in the corp, if everyone spammed corp mail for every little thing it would turn into a mess very quickly.
